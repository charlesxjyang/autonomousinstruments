Collaboration for Autonomous Science Instruments

A workshop convening scientists, instrument vendors, and AI-for-Science startups to collaborate on better software and interoperability for autonomous laboratories.

When

October 23–24, 2025

Where

San Diego, California

Organizer

Charles Yang
AI for Science Fellow at Renaissance Philanthropy

About

This workshop aims to catalyze collaborations between researchers, startups, and science instrument vendors to accelerate autonomous lab development.
